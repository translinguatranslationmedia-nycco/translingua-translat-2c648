What is the process for getting my foreign academic credentials evaluated and translated?
To begin, email or bring your original academic documents (such as diplomas, transcripts, or certificates) to our Manhattan office or submit them securely via our website. You can choose to pick up your documents in person or have them mailed to your NYC address.
